About Us / من نحن

ZNI International Drones Cleaning LLC is a specialized drone solutions company based in the UAE. We supply advanced drones designed for exterior cleaning and agricultural use, and we provide access to certified drone operators for clients who require on-site services. With a strong focus on innovation, safety, and efficiency, we help businesses and property owners achieve high-standard cleaning without traditional labor.
